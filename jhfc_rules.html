  • Snake Draft-14 Rounds

Standings

  • One division
  • Seeded by Victory Points
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. Adjusted Record
      2. Total Points For
  • Victory Points (VP):
    • Winning your weekly matchup=2 VP
    • Top 5 in total points for the week=2 VP
    • Middle 5 in total points for the week=1 VP

Schedule

  • 15 week regular season
  • Play everyone once

Roster

  • 4 Bench slots
  • 2 IR slot
  • Once a player plays for the day, they are locked into their daily slot

Playoffs

  • 8 Teams make the playoffs
    • 1 week per playoff round

Waivers

  • $100 free agent auction budget for the season
  • $1 minimum bid
  • Waivers process once a week at 7pm (central time) on Sunday

Side Pots

  • Team with the most total points for the week wins the designated side pot amount
  • Side pot $ amount is the same for every week
  • Side pot only active during regular season
