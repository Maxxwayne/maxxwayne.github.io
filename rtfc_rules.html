Draft

  • Auction Draft-$200 Budget
  • Nomination order randomized 1 hour before the draft
  • 14 Rounds
  • Date/Time: 9/2 @7pm central

Standings

  • One division
  • Seeded by Victory Points
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. Adjusted Record
      2. Total Points For
  • Victory Points (VP):
    • Winning your weekly matchup=2 VP
    • Top 1/3 (top 4) in total points for the week=2 VP
    • Middle 1/3 (5-8) in total points for the week=1 VP

Schedule

  • 11 week regular season
  • Play everyone once

Roster

  • Starting Rosters: QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, TE, 2 FLEX (RB/WR/TE), 1 individual defensive player
  • 5 Bench slots
  • 1 IR slot
  • Once a player plays for the week, they are locked into their slot

Playoffs

  • 6 Teams make the playoffs
    • 1 and 2 seed get a bye
    • First round is 1 week
    • Semifinals and Finals are each 2 weeks

Waivers

  • $110 free agent auction budget for the season
  • $1 minimum bid
  • Waivers process at noon (central time) on Wednesday
  • If a player is not claimed on waivers, they become a free agent
    • Free Agents are free and are “first come first serve”

Scoring

  • .5 PPR
  • See Scoring tab for more details

Side Pots

  • Team with the most total points for the week wins the designated side pot amount
  • Side pot $ amount is the same for every week
  • Side pot only active during regular season

Keepers

  • Every team has the option of 1 keeper from the previous season
  • To be an eligible keeper, they must be drafted and held onto for the entire previous season by your team
  • Keeper will cost $10+what you paid for him last draft
    • This amount will be subtracted from your $200 draft budget before the draft begins
