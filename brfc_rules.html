Draft

  • Snake-22 rounds
  • Draft order randomized an hour before the draft
  • 90 seconds per pick
  • Draft date/time: July 20th-8pm Central

Standings

  • Three Divisions-6 Teams per division
  • Victory Points (VP):
    • Winning your weekly matchup=2 VP
    • Top 6 in total points for the week=2 VP
    • Middle 6 in total points for the week=1 VP

Schedule

  • 16 week regular season
    • Play everyone in your division twice
    • WPlay 3 opponents in each of other divisions
  • Each week (excluding week 1 and All-Star Week) starts Monday and goes through games on Sunday
  • Playoffs are 2 weeks per round

Rosters

  • Starting Rosters: C, 1B, 2B, SS, 3B, IF, 4 OF, Utility, 4 SP, and 2 RP
  • 5 Bench slots
  • 2 IR slots
  • Once a player plays for the week, they are locked in for the week

Playoffs

  • 6 teams make the playoffs
  • Best Record in each division are top 3 seeds
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. VP
      2. Adjusted Record (what your record would be if you played everyone every week)
      3. Total Points For
    • They are ranked 1-3 by VP
      • 1 and 2 get a first round bye
  • 3 Wild Card slots are based on most VP to not win a division
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. Adjusted Record (what your record would be if you played everyone every week)
      2. Total Points For
  • 2 weeks per playoff round
  • Reseed Playoffs after first round

Waivers

  • $100 free agent budget for the season
    • Trading Free Agent $ is allowed
    • Minimum bid of $1
    • Waivers go through at Noon (central time) on Sunday

    Sidepots

    • Team with the most total points for the week wins the designated side pot amount
    • Side pot $ amount is the same for every week
    • Side pot only active during regular season
