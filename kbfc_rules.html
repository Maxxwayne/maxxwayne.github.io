Standings

  • Three Divisions-8 teams per division
  • Victory Points(VP):
    • Earn 2 VP if you win your weekly matchup
    • Earn 2 VP if you're in the top 1/3 (top 8) in points for the week
    • Earn 1 VP if you're in the middle 1/3 (9-16) in points for the week
  • Divisions are seeded by record
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. Victory Points
      2. Adjusted Record
      3. Total Points For
  • Combined Standings are seeded by VP
    • Tiebreakers:
      1. Adjusted Record
      2. Total Points For

    Schedule

    • 13 week regular season
    • Play everyone in your division once and 3 from each of the other 2 divisions

    Roster

    • Starting Rosters: QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, TE, 2 FLEX (RB/WR/TE), 1 individual defensive player
    • 5 Bench slots
    • 1 IR slot
    • Once a player plays for the week, they are locked into their slot

    Playoffs

    • 8 Teams make the playoffs
      • Division winners are top 3 seeds
        • They are ranked by 1-3 by VP
      • 5 Wild Cards
        • Criteria:
          1. Victory Points
          2. Adjusted Record
          3. Total Points For

    Waivers

    • $110 free agent auction budget for the season
    • $1 minimum bid
    • Waivers process at noon (central time) on Wednesday
    • If a player is not claimed on waivers, they become a free agent
      • Free Agents are free and are “first come first serve”

    Scoring

    • .5 PPR
    • .5 Rushing/Recieving 1st Down
    • See Scoring tab for more details

    Side Pots

    • Team with the most total points for the week wins the designated side pot amount
    • Side pot $ amount is the same for every week
    • Side pot only active during regular season
